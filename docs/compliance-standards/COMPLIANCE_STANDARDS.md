## 1. What Regulations Does This System Handle?

This platform is designed to check bank compliance against:

| Regulation | Authority | Description |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| Cabinet Decision No. 74 | UAE Government | Combatting Terrorism Financing, WMD Proliferation |
| AML/CFT Regulations | UAE Central Bank (CBUAE) | Anti-Money Laundering, Counter Financing of Terrorism |
| UN Security Council Resolutions | United Nations | Sanctions, Targeted Financial Sanctions (TFS) |
| FATF Recommendations | FATF | 40 Recommendations for AML/CFT frameworks |
| Federal Decree-Law No. 20/2018 | UAE Government | On Anti-Money Laundering |

---

## 2. Compliance Levels Explained

### ✅ Compliant
- The bank's internal process document FULLY addresses the regulatory requirement
- Evidence clearly maps to the requirement
- No further action needed

### ⚠️ Partial Compliant  
- The bank's internal process document PARTIALLY addresses the requirement
- Some aspects are covered but gaps exist
- Requires corrective action with timeline

### ❌ Non-Compliant
- The bank's internal process document does NOT address the requirement at all
- No matching policy/procedure found
- Requires immediate action plan
