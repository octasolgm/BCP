# 📱 BCP - User Guide
**Version:** 1.0
**Date:** 2026-06-22

---

## 1. Getting Started

### 1.1 Login
1. Open the web app URL in your browser (Chrome/Edge recommended)
2. Enter your email and password provided by your administrator
3. Click "Login"

### 1.2 Dashboard (Home Screen)
After login, you see the **Compliance Dashboard** showing:
- 🟢 **Compliant items** (percentage and count)
- 🟡 **Partial Compliant items** (percentage and count)
- 🔴 **Non-Compliant items** (percentage and count)
- 📊 **Charts** showing trends over time
- ⚠️ **Overdue actions** requiring attention

---

## 2. Running a Compliance Check

### Step 1: Upload Requirement File
1. Click **"New Analysis"** button
2. In the **"Requirement Document"** section, drag and drop your regulatory PDF
   (e.g., Cabinet Decision No. 74)
3. Supported formats: PDF, Word, HTML, JPEG, PNG

### Step 2: Upload Internal Documents
1. In the **"Internal Process Documents"** section, upload your bank's policy files
2. You can upload multiple internal documents
3. Supported formats: PDF, Word, Excel, plain text

### Step 3: Start Analysis
1. Click **"Compare & Analyze"**
2. Wait for the AI to process (typically 1-3 minutes)
3. A progress bar shows the current status

### Step 4: Review Results
1. Results appear in a table/grid format
2. Each row shows:
   - **Requirement text** (what the law says)
   - **Internal document match** (where your bank covers it)
   - **Status badge**: 🟢 Compliant | 🟡 Partial | 🔴 Non-Compliant

### Step 5: Take Action on Gaps
1. Click on any 🟡 or 🔴 item
2. Fill in:
   - **Responsible Person** (who will fix this?)
   - **Target Date** (when will it be fixed?)
   - **Action Plan** (how will it be fixed?)
3. Click **"Save"**

### Step 6: Download Excel Report
1. Click **"Export to Excel"** button
2. A formatted Excel file downloads matching your bank's template

---

## 3. Fixing Non-Compliant Items

### Upload Corrective Document
1. Go to **"Action Tracker"** page
2. Find the non-compliant item
3. Click **"Upload Remediation"**
4. Upload the updated policy document
5. System will re-analyze and update status to 🟢 if the gap is addressed

---

## 4. Mobile App

### What Can I Do On Mobile?
- ✅ View compliance dashboard
- ✅ See my assigned tasks/actions
- ✅ Upload a corrective document (take photo or select file)
- ✅ Receive push notifications for:
  - Upcoming target dates (3 days before)
  - Missed deadlines (immediate alert)
  - New actions assigned to you

---

## 5. Alerts & Notifications
The system automatically sends alerts when:
- ⏰ A target date is approaching (3 days before deadline)
- 🚨 A target date is MISSED (overdue)
- 📋 A new action is assigned to you
- ✅ An item's status changes to Compliant

Alerts appear:
- As push notifications on your mobile phone
- As email to your registered email address
- As banners in the web dashboard
